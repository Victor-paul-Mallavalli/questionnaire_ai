Security Questionnaire for Vendor Assessment

1. What encryption standards do you use for data at rest and in transit?
2. How do you ensure HIPAA compliance in your data handling processes?
3. What authentication methods do you support for user access?
4. Describe your incident response plan for data breaches.
5. How often do you conduct security audits and vulnerability assessments?
6. What is your data backup and disaster recovery strategy?
7. How do you handle data retention and deletion procedures?
8. What measures do you take to secure API endpoints?
9. Describe your employee security training and background check procedures.
10. What third-party integrations do you support and how are they secured?
